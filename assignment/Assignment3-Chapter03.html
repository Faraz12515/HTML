Muffin Recipe

A basic recipe. You can add pretty much anything to it!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 200g caster sugar
  • 250ml milk
  • 125ml vegetable oil
  • 400g plain flour
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt

Method

  1. Preheat oven to Gas Mark 6 or a medium temperature.Line muffin tray with paper muffin cases.
  2. Beat the eggs in a mixing bowl and then mix in the oil and milk.Add the sugar and whisk unitil dissolved.
  3. Shift the dry ingrediants: flour,baking powder and slat and mix until smooth.NB: add any extra ingrediants now eg. chocolate chips/fruit.
  4. Fill muffin cases TWO THIRDS full (this is important for the perfect size muffins) and bake in the middle of the oven for 25 minutes (approx, depending on your oven).
  5. Leave to cool (or enjoy warm!).
